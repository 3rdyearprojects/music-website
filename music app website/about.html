About Us

Our Mission

At MusicWeb, our mission is to make it easy for everyone to discover and enjoy music from across the globe. We believe in the power of music to bring people together and strive to create a platform that reflects the diversity and creativity of music creators and fans alike.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by a group of music enthusiasts and technologists, MusicWeb started as a small project aimed at helping friends share and discover new music. Since then, we've grown into a global platform with millions of songs, podcasts, and videos from artists all over the world.
